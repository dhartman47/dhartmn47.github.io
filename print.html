Individual Design Project

Group Print Project

For this assignment we were instructed to make a bookmark that elementary students would be interested in. The constraints for the bookmark were it had to be a 2in x 8in size, it made to be one color, all text and font were compatible, and line art only. When first given the project we were instructed to make 5 designs ourselves and then pick 1 out of the 5. Once we chose our final design we had to create a final sketch. After that you move to the computer, we used the software of InDesign. While using InDesign I learned how to properly edit and place images and lines where they needed to be. Then once I was finished with all the editing we moved to actually printing out the bookmark. During that process I learned how to use different machines, we learned how to use the printing press and how to use the paper cutter. Overall, the assignment was very fun to make!
