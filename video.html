Video Project on Youtube

During this project I worked with Jocelyn and Kaelyn to make a video based on the Pond at Millersville University. We chose to make our video lighthearted and fun. We still gave information about the swans and where the pond was but we decided that we were going to use the swans as our enemy since many students have been attacked by them over the years. Before we started to actually record our video we created a script and storyboard. They gave us a rough layout of how we want the video to go. Then after we got that approved we started to record. To do so, we took a walk down to the pond where we chose to film. During filming we use wireless, bluetooth mics and an Iphone 13 pro to record. Afterward we edited the video and uploaded it to youtube when we felt we were done. This project was probably one of my favorites from this class.
